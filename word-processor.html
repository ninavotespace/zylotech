Introduction

Welcome to ZyloDocs Pro. This is a fully featured word processor running entirely in your browser. It supports rich text formatting, tables, images, and code blocks.

Key Features:

  • All Word Fonts: Arial, Times New Roman, Calibri, Garamond, plus 40+ Google Fonts.
  • Layout Control: Adjust line spacing and page margins instantly.
  • Export to Word: Downloads as .doc compatible with MS Word.
  • PDF Export: High-quality print-ready PDFs.
  • Auto-Save: Your work is saved locally in real-time.
  • Find & Replace: Search and replace text throughout your document.
  • Word Count: Detailed statistics about your document.
  • Complete Font Size Range: 6pt to 72pt + custom sizes.
  • Upload Support: Import Word, PDF, HTML, and TXT files.
